Why chose to work with Us?

Our Speciales are Premium Irish Oysters from Magilligan Strand, known for their sweet taste and high meat content.

Physical Benefits

High meat content in our Oysters due to natural alluvium from natural river and mineral rich glacial depositation on the seabed and deltas

Quick growth

Oysters benefit from little baring which promotes quick growth. But also the natural movements of tides therefore less Physical shaking out of the water thus less trama to younger spawn

Experience

Over 30 yrs in the Irish oyster industry including dredging. Have full view on the benefit of Sustainable farming and less impact on the sea bed and marine life

Sustainability

Natual feeding only, no chemicals or feed required due to natural flow of alluvium from the Iron rich River Roe as , boosting the natural iron rich premium oysters and promoting health bulk growth
